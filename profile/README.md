## Overview

<a href="#">DxO FilmPack</a> recreates the visual characteristics of analog photographic films through scientific measurement rather than approximation. DxO laboratories physically measured each included film stock — analyzing how different emulsions respond to light, render colors, produce grain, and handle tonal transitions. These measurements power film simulations that produce the actual look of shooting on that specific film rather than a generic vintage filter effect.

The <a href="#">film profile library</a> covers over 80 color and black-and-white film stocks from manufacturers including Kodak, Fujifilm, Ilford, Agfa, and others. Each profile includes the specific color science, grain structure, and tonal response of that emulsion at its rated ISO. <a href="#">Grain simulation</a> renders film grain that matches the actual grain pattern of each stock — Kodak Portra's fine grain looks different from Ilford Delta's crystalline structure, and FilmPack reproduces both accurately.

<a href="#">Color rendering adjustments</a> control how the film profile interacts with your source image. The intensity slider blends between the original digital capture and the full film look, enabling subtle or strong application of each profile. <a href="#">Developer settings</a> for black-and-white films change how the emulsion was processed, producing variations in contrast and tonal separation that different darkroom chemicals would create on the original film.

The <a href="#">creative tools</a> add additional analog photographic effects beyond film simulation. Vignetting reproduces lens and film falloff with natural-looking edge darkening. Light leaks simulate the flare and color wash that occurs when film is accidentally exposed to light. <a href="#">Texture overlays</a> add aged paper and film surface effects. These tools combine with film profiles to create complete analog photographic aesthetics rather than just color grading.

## Key Features

- <a href="#">80+ measured film profiles</a> reproduce Kodak Fuji Ilford and Agfa emulsions with scientific accuracy
- <a href="#">Accurate grain simulation</a> matches the specific crystal structure of each film stock at its rated ISO
- <a href="#">Color science reproduction</a> captures how each emulsion uniquely responds to different light and subjects
- <a href="#">Developer simulation</a> for black-and-white films changes contrast based on darkroom processing variations
- <a href="#">Blend intensity control</a> mixes the original digital image with the film profile at any strength
- <a href="#">Creative analog effects</a> add authentic vignetting light leaks and aged film texture overlays
- <a href="#">Zone system controls</a> adjust highlight midtone and shadow distribution with darkroom-style precision
- <a href="#">Lightroom and Photoshop plugins</a> embed FilmPack directly into your existing editing application workflow
- <a href="#">Batch film application</a> applies consistent profiles across entire shoots for cohesive aesthetic results
